About us


Our mission at RightPet is to let humans to coexist with dogs in the most natural way. RightPet is the new all-in-one solution for adopting rescued dogs. The dogs should be able to to find the right home using RightPet. To achieve this, we try our best to contact every local shelter in the area, but sometimes the timing just doesn't work out. That's why we give you the chance to help us find new homes for animals across the U.K. (addresses, phone numbers and pet information for every shelter in the U.K. on our pet waiting list).
