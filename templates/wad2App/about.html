About us



This app was built by a group of students from the University of Glasgow, for the Web Application Development 2 module.

It represents a fictious dog shelter, where people can:

  • browse through and filter profiles of the dogs currenly in the shelter
  • create a user profile
  • keep a list of favouite dogs
  • schedule visits to the shelter
  • initiate adoption requests
  • contact the staff

Staff can:

  • keep track of applications via a dashboard
  • keep track and schedule all visits to the shelter via a calendar view
  • manage dog profiles
  • contact users
